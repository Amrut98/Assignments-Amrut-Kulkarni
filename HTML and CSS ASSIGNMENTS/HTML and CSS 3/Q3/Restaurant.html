Sanjeev Kapoor

Famous for his TV show called Khana Khazana, Sanjeev Kapoor has easily become one of the most famous Indian celebrity chefs all over the world. His show ran for more than 17 years. He has written over 150 best selling cookbooks, is a restaurateur and winner of several culinary awards too.


Vikas Khanna

Chef Khanna is one of the most renowned Indian chefs. Although he now lives in New York and has earned a Michelin star for his restaurant, Junoon, he has worked in India for quite some time with many hotel chains like the Taj, Oberoi, Welcome, and Leela Group of Hotels. He has earned many awards including GQ India Man Of The Year, 2012.
